About This Website

This little website lets you learn about the various musical instrumeents that make up a modern symphony orchestra.

All of the information about each instrument is stored in a database on the server. If you log into this website with your Google Plus credentials, you can add, modify and delete the instruments in the database.

Choose a section from the list on the left to see the instruments in that section of the orchestra.
